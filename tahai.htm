The Story

In my second year in Secondary education i started to ponder about a game idea about a man thrown in to arena prison that would have to fight to keep alive. Much of the idea came from the first Fable and probably the movie Gladiator.

At the start of the third and final year we needed to make a project to get our diploma, and my arena idea got the vote. We didn't come far but at the end of the year we had a working game with a menu and everything, and it was also actually kind of fun.

We only managed to create a half-assed combat system and bad enemy ai but we learned alot. Would i try make the exact same game today i would probably only need a day or two.


Whats Next

I still kind of like the idea so i might remake it better with more content some time in the future. i will probably have to remake every aspect of the game, but it would be fun.


What would I improve now

I would start by remaking the combar and movement and then try to implement an AI system like a Finite State Machine and build the AI from there.
