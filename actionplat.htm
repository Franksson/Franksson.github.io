The Story

So i had finished my secondary education and wanted a new project to tackle. Becuse i recently had been playing a lot of Zelda i wanted to try to make a game like that.

I started by creating a village and some npc to talk to, the dialog was a bit of a challange and the result i came up with was a bit too complicated, but it worked. I also kind of created a quest for the player to finish.

I then realized that the true fun was in the dungeons and what better way to prototype the game than creating a dungeon and puzzle mechanics. I stopped working on the game when i realized i needed to study up alot on level design.


Whats Next

If i were to continue on the game i would probably work with a friend with the level design on the dungeon and create a story line to take the player from the village to the dungeon.


What would I improve now

I would remake the dialog and quest system to be more simple and expandeble, i would remake the inventory system and tweak the jumping to act more like a smooth feeling platfomer.
