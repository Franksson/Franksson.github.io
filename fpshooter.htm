The Story

I have always been a Counter-Strike player. I really loved how the guns felt in the latest installation of the series, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive.

Trying to creating similar guns in Unity felt like a fun and rewarding challange. I already knew that CS guns was hitscan but the real challange was creating good recoil.

After a day or two i felt that i have created a prettty good and expandable system, and the makeshift animations i made always gives me a laugh.


Whats Next

I could add multiplayer to the game and make some deathmatch kind of thing with it, but making a single player campain with dialog and enemy ai seems more fun to me. If I'm going to expand on this project I will probably try to make it a intense zombie shooting game with some story to it.
